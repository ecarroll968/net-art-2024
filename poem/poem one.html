I absolutely adore observing a stunning and breathtaking sunset, when summer is drifting towards the fall. Ruby red tinged clouds
I can not
forget - they are the most
sight of them all!

Twilight has arrived, to another day I must bid adieu

as scarlet skies transform


to an inky and  velvety blu

Why the sunset is my favorite

  1. It is bright
  2. It is beautiful
  3. It is calming
  4. It is memorable
There are other reasons why I love the sunset and when seasons change
  • The cool fall breeze feels amazing
  • I enjoy apple cider
  • I get to spend time with my family because of increased holidays
  • I am able to wear more sweaters
Fall is amazing!
